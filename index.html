About Me

Hello, my name is Monika! Originally from Bulgaria, but have been a Charlotte girl for the last 3 years. I was raised in a family of artists, so I decided to follow my parents steps and graduated Graphic Design in 2009. Right after, I felt that I need some extra challenge and went to law school. That was a fun ride! Moved to US in 2012 and have been stay-at-home mom for the last 3 years.

I like to cook, I love being outdoors and driving long distance. Any kind of art truly excites me, and so does problem-solving. Coding combines both worlds for me makes me totally in love with writing code! Full stack developer in progress.
